Refund & Cancellation Policy

At JetSwiftFly, we facilitate flight and hotel bookings. Our refund policy is as follows:

  • Refunds for cancellations will be processed according to the airline or hotel's specific terms.
  • Approved refunds will be credited back to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.
  • A service fee of 10% may apply to cancellations made by the user.
